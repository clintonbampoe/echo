# Product Functions (Updated)

## Core Accounting

- **Income Management**
  
  - Record inflows into the system.
  - Group income into designated categories.
  - Support multiple sources (services, events, contributions).
  - Organize records for smooth analysis and reporting.

- **Expenditure Management**
  
  - Track all outflows.
  - Group expenditures into categories.
  - Link expenditures to projects or designated purposes.
  - Organize records for smooth analysis and reporting.

- **Cashbook Reconciliation**
  
  - Maintain balances across multiple accounts.
  - Reconcile receipts and payments with account records.
  - Handle adjustments, charges, and closing balances.

---

## Contributions

- **Contribution Tracking**
  
  - Record individual member contributions.
  - Support periodic tracking (weekly, monthly, yearly).
  - Organize contributions for analysis and reporting.

- **Tithe Management**
  
  - Record tithes separately from other contributions.
  - Support member-linked tracking for accountability.
  - Provide periodic summaries (monthly, yearly) for transparency.
  - Enable aggregation for reporting at society, circuit, or diocese level.

- **Organizational Dues**
  
  - Track dues across fellowships or groups.
  - Compare expected vs. actual contributions.
  - Generate contribution summaries per member group.

---

## Projects

- **Project Fund Management**
  
  - Allocate and monitor funds for initiatives.
  - Record payments and advances related to projects.
  - Track progress against budget and completion stages.

- **Fundraising Integration**
  
  - Link special appeals or donations to projects.
  - Track pledges and fulfillment status.

---

## Assets

- **Asset Register**
  
  - Maintain records of physical and financial assets.
  - Track acquisition details, valuation, and ownership.
  - Support updates for depreciation or revaluation.

- **Welfare & Designated Funds**
  
  - Manage funds earmarked for welfare or special purposes.
  - Track disbursements and maintain balances.

---

## Reporting

- **Financial Statements**
  
  - Generate structured statements (Receipts & Payments, Trial Balance, Cash Book, Balance Sheet).
  - Provide summaries of income, expenditure, and balances.

- **Analytical Reports**
  
  - Produce periodic income and expenditure analysis.
  - Generate member contribution and dues analysis.
  - Provide project expenditure vs. budget comparisons.
  - Support external reporting requirements (circuit/diocese returns).

- **Audit & Export**
  
  - Enable export in standard formats (PDF, Excel, CSV).
  - Maintain complete audit logs to ensure transparency and accountability.
    - This means: **"Keeping detailed audit logs that record all actions and decisions. These logs make processes transparent by showing what happened, when, and by whom, and they uphold accountability by providing evidence that individuals or teams can be held responsible for their actions."**

---

## Attendance

- **Service & Event Tracking**
  - Record attendance across services and events.
  - Link attendance data to contribution and participation patterns.
  - Generate statistics for engagement analysis.
