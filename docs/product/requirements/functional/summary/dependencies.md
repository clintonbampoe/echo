# Dependencies of Functional Requirements

This document outlines how each functional module depends on or feeds into others. It provides a system‑wide view of data flow and accountability.

---

## Core Accounting

**Depends on:**

- Contributions → inflows from member giving (tithes, dues, offerings).

- Projects → expenditures linked to initiatives.

- Assets → acquisition/disposal of assets affects balances.

**Feeds into:**

- Reporting → financial statements and summaries.

---

## Contributions

**Depends on:**

- Membership Management → contributions must be linked to members or groups for accountability.

**Feeds into:**

- Core Accounting → contributions are recorded as income.

- Reporting → contribution analysis and summaries.

---

## Projects

**Depends on:**

- Core Accounting → project expenditures and allocations.

- Contributions → special fundraising or pledges tied to projects.

**Feeds into:**

- Reporting → budget vs. expenditure comparisons.

- Assets → project‑related acquisitions.

---

## Assets

**Depends on:**

- Core Accounting → acquisition and disposal transactions.

**Feeds into:**

- Reporting → asset registers and designated fund balances.

---

## Reporting

**Depends on:**

- Core Accounting → income, expenditure, balances.

- Contributions → member and group giving analysis.

- Projects → expenditure vs. budget.

- Assets → asset registers and fund balances.

- Attendance → engagement statistics.

- Membership Management → member/group linkage for contribution and attendance analysis.

**Feeds into:**

- External stakeholders (circuit, diocese, auditors).

---

## Attendance

**Depends on:**

- Membership Management → linking attendance to members and groups.

**Feeds into:**

- Reporting → engagement analysis.

- Contributions (stretch) → correlation between attendance and giving.

---

## Membership Management

**Depends on:**

- None (foundational).

**Feeds into:**

- Contributions → member‑linked giving.

- Attendance → member participation.

- Reporting → member/group accountability.

- Projects → member/group involvement.
